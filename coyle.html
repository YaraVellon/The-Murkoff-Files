Coyle

Character Summary

Coyle is a cunning and manipulative Prime Asset, known for his intelligence and tactical mind. Originally a field operative for Murkoff, he became a Prime Asset through experimental reagents that enhanced his perception, reflexes, and problem-solving under stress. Coyle prefers to outsmart his targets rather than overpower them.

Behavior in the Trials

In the Trials, Coyle focuses on ambushes, traps, and psychological warfare. He tends to avoid direct confrontation unless the odds are in his favor. His keen sense of observation allows him to predict Reagents' movements, making him highly effective at controlling the flow of the Trial. Coyle’s attacks are precise and calculated, striking fear without warning.

How to Play Against Coyle

  1. Stay unpredictable: Coyle anticipates repetitive patterns. Change routes and tactics frequently.
  2. Check for traps: He sets subtle traps in key areas—careful observation is crucial.
  3. Use distractions: Noisy devices or misdirections can force him to reposition.
  4. Coordinate with teammates: Splitting attention can confuse him, but avoid isolation.
  5. Observe and learn: Coyle’s behavior is tactical; understanding his ambush patterns is key to survival.
  6. Don’t rush objectives: Patience and careful timing reduce risk against his calculated attacks.
