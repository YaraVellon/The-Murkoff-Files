Otto

Character Summary

Otto is a highly unpredictable Prime Asset known for his brutal efficiency and terrifying presence in the Trials. Originally a leading researcher in the Murkoff Corporation's experimental programs, he became an asset after repeated exposure to unknown reagents that enhanced his reflexes and aggression. Otto rarely speaks and moves with a calculated precision that unsettles even the most experienced Reagents.

Behavior in the Trials

In the Trials, Otto exhibits extreme territorial behavior. He actively patrols key areas, setting traps and ambushing players who linger too long. Unlike other Prime Assets, he is highly aware of Reagents' presence and can anticipate their movements. Otto prefers close-quarters combat and is exceptionally fast, making him one of the deadliest threats if approached carelessly.

How to Play Against Otto

  1. Keep your distance: Otto excels in melee range. Always maintain a safe distance when possible.
  2. Watch for traps: He sets traps around high-value objectives. Use remote abilities or tools to detect them.
  3. Use distractions: Loud noises and thrown devices can lure him away from key paths, giving your team time to advance.
  4. Team coordination: Split attention can confuse Otto, but never separate too far. He reacts quickly to isolated Reagents.
  5. Observe patterns: Otto has predictable patrols; learning these routes can help you avoid direct confrontation.
  6. Prepare to retreat: If you engage, have an exit plan. Otto is relentless and can corner unprepared players quickly.
