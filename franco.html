Franco

Character Summary

Franco is a relentless and imposing Prime Asset, designed to dominate physically and psychologically. Once a former security specialist within Murkoff, he underwent extensive enhancement via reagents to amplify his strength, endurance, and combat instincts. His presence in the Trials is intimidating, and his brutal tactics make him a force to be reckoned with.

Behavior in the Trials

Franco is aggressive and highly territorial. He actively pursues Reagents, favoring direct confrontations and using brute force to control key areas. Franco is less subtle than other Prime Assets, relying on intimidation and raw power to overwhelm his targets. His attacks are devastating and often leave Reagents with minimal time to react.

How to Play Against Franco

  1. Keep your distance: Franco’s strength is overwhelming in close range. Always try to maintain space.
  2. Use obstacles: Doors, walls, and tight corners can slow him down and give you breathing room.
  3. Distract him: Throwing objects or activating alarms can divert his attention temporarily.
  4. Attack in numbers: Franco struggles when multiple Reagents coordinate attacks, making teamwork essential.
  5. Know escape routes: Always plan a fallback path. Franco’s relentless pursuit can trap unprepared Reagents quickly.
  6. Patience is key: Don’t rush objectives; observe his patrols and time your movements carefully.
